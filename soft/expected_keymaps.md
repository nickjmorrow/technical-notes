close tab: ctrl+w
new tab: ctrl+t
close application: ctrl+q
new window: ctrl+n
save: ctrl+s
save as: ctrl+shift+s
to right tab: ctrl+tab
to left tab: ctrl+shift+tab
reload: ctrl+r
open smart bar or nav bar: ctrl+p
find: ctrl+f
undo: ctrl+z
copy: ctrl+c
cut: ctrl+x
paste: ctrl+v
build: ctrl+b
toggle comment: ctrl+/
delete line: ctrl+shift+k
